Actionable tips:

1. Get a feel-good physical journal
2. Use a journaling app too (you can copy or photo your physical journal pages into the digital system)
3. Get a list of prompts
4. Talk to AI, ask for interesting prompts
5. Distinguish between daily habit and deep dive journaling
    1. daily habit: 5-10 minutes
    2. deep dive: dedicate a few hours, go to a new place, take some prompts and a notebook with you - do a mini retreat
