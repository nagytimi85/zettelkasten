Excellent article with a tons of reading recommendations. 

What I don't like: to call every notes collection a Zettelkasten. I prefer if we use the German, we should use it to describe the Luhmannian notes collection. 
- I understand tho what Chris says, that maybe we are witnessing a process similar to Kleenex becoming kleenex, Xerox becoming xerox, or for Hungarians, Cellux becoming cellux, Tesa becoming téza, etc.
