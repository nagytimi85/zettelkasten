[[ref-alderman-the-power-2017|The Power]] by [[ref-alderman-naomi|Naomi Alderman]] shows an example of how to create matriarchy in fiction. 

In this story, a genetical mutation appears in a generation of women:
- women gain unexpected physical advantage through the awakening of a sleeping gene in a generation of young women, making them able to generate electric pulses by their hands that can harm and even kill
