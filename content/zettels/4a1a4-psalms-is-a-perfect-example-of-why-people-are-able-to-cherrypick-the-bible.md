Psalms is a perfect example of why people are able to cherrypick the Bible and shape it to fit vastly different and even contradicting world views. 

Side by side, you can find beautiful prayers and horrific curses, and you can pick and choose, based on how you picture the god you want to speak to:
- a loving, supporting, sustaining, nourishing force, or
- a vengeful, merciless, cruel authority.
