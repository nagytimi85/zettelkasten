## What is my favorite part of the Bible

- "Christian" literally means "a follower of Christ".
- So I can argue that as a Christian, you need to look at a Bible starting at the Gospels.
	- The Gospels are recollections of the life and teachings of Jesus the Christ, in 4 different versions.
- Within the Gospels, I choose to look at the Sermon on the Mount (Hegyi beszéd) as my favorite part.

## My arguments for the Sermon on the Mount

- The Sermon on the Mount contains the teachings of Jesus
- It is the largest comprehensive part of the Bible containing the teachings of Jesus

- I think that if we aim for nothing else but just to follow the teachings of the Sermon on the Mount
	- a) we aim for a virtuous life
	- b) we are supplied with good moral challenges for a lifetime 
	- = meaning: it is both good and enough
