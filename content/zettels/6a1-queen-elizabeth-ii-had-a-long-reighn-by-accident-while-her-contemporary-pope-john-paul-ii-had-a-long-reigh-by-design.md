I checked once when Queen Elizabeth II was still alive: she was already the queen when my parents were born, and I’m 40 now (as of 2025). No wonder generations thought of her being a constant.

She rose to the throne so young (at only 25) because her father died at only 56. 

Her reign was so long by accident, while her contemporary, [[6a-john-paul-ii-was-the-third-longest-reigning-pope-not-by-accident-but-by-strategy|Pope John Paul II had a long papal reign by design]].
