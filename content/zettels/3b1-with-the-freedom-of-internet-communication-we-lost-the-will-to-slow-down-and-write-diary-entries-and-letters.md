With the freedom of communication, brought upon by the rise of modern technology, our interactions via the internet became more frequent but also more superficial. 

We generally lost two very important practices to organise our inner world and help our understanding of the outer world: 
- keepig a diary and
- corresponding 

These are common ways to slow down and make room for deeper thinking.
