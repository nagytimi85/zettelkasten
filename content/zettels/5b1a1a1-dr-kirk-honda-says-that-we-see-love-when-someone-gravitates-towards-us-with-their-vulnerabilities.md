[[ref-honda-dr-kirk|Dr. Kirk Honda]] says that especially in early stages of a relationship, when we search for indications of love, we are looking for a tendency towards us when it comes to share vulnerability, a tendency that sets us apart from others. If he loves me, he would want to share sadness, joy, hurt, affection, worry with me first, these vulnerabilities gravitate towards me. 

Source: [Love Is Blind S9 #47 - Therapist Reacts](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TKAC-wPEjZo)
