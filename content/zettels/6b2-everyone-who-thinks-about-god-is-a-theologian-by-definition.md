[[ref-womach-faith|Faith Womach]] of the [[ref-bible-nerd-ministries|Bible Nerd Ministries]] said in [one of her videos](https://youtu.be/UpYxXQutcGI?si=9DBtYkS-iQIO_s91) that every one of us is a theologian by definition. Theologian means a person who is thinking about God. 

So although there are formally trained theologians, professional theologians, armchair theologians, hobby theologians, by definition, everyone who is pondering about God is a theologian of some kind.
