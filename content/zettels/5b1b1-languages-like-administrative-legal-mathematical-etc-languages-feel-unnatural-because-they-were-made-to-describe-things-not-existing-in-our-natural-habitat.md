In contrast to everyday human language, [[ref-harari-yuval-noah|Harari]] mentions in his book [[ref-harari-sapiens-2015|Sapiens]] the language and thinking of administrators (which I am!) and bureaucrats. 

- Gossip-theory says that the human language feels like it developed especially for "gossiping", for exchanging intel about people. [[5b1b-gossip-theory-says-that-the-human-language-feels-like-it-developed-especially-for-gossiping]]

Languages like administrative, bureaucratic, legal, mathematical, computer science etc. language feel totally different than natural language. We had to invent, design these in order to describe systems not occurring in our natural habitat, where we mostly needed to be concerned about botanical and social information.
