[[ref-honda-dr-kirk|Dr. Kirk Honda]] shared the intel that often when a couple is presented on Love is Blind as forever sweethearts who were only interested in each other from day one, also were in love triangle, love square, love polygon situations. But since their other connections didn't make it to the next phase, it made more sense from an editing perspective to let those other plot lines wither.
