Most of western art is either Bible- or Greek mythology fan fiction or fan art.

Just think about it for a moment: what would be left of western art, music, literature, theatre, architecture and culture, if we would take away all the Bible and Greek mythology fan fiction and fan art? 

If we took these away, we (at least we in the West) couldn't recognise the past and present around us.
