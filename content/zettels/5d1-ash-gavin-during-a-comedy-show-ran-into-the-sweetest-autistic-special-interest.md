Ash Gavin had a wholesome convo with an autistic women in her audience about special interest.

Gavin: This is a delightful crowd. 
Women in the crowd: And I'm autistic!!!
G: ... Yes, you are. (...) Okay, I love you so much. (...) You're a Pixar character. They're gonna make a Pixar movie called Neuro-love-able and they're gonna put you, dressed in the overalls, with the service dog... (...)
G: So what's your special interest?
W: Children's health care!!!
G: Children's health care... That is the sweetest special interest. I love autistic women. Men are just like... the sea! Boats! Dungeons and Dragons!
