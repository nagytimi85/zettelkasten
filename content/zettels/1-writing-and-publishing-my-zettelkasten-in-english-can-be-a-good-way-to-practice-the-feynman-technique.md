# The Feynman-technique urges you to learn by

1. Teaching a topic to yourself
2. Teaching the topic to someone else, optimally in a simple way (like teaching it to a 10 year old)
3. Identify the gaps of your understanding 
4. Keep learning, refining and simplifying your knowledge


## Publishing is a mindset of teaching

Publishing my thoughts facilitate the mindset of teaching, explaining. 


## Writing in English makes me simplify

Since English is my second language, using it makes me simplify my message.
