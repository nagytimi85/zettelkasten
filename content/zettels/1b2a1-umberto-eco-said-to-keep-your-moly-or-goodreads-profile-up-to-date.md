[[ref-eco-umberto|Umberto Eco]] suggests in his book [[ref-eco-how-to-write-a-thesis|How to Write a Thesis]] to keep your [moly.hu](http://moly.hu) database up-to-date. 

Alright, of course he didn't say that in the '70s in Italy.

But he recommended to save at least a short review, maybe a few quotes and reading notes. You'll never know when it will be useful. 

This is what the "én és a könyv" (me and the book) page on "the Hungarian GoodReads", [moly.hu](http://moly.hu) can do for you.
