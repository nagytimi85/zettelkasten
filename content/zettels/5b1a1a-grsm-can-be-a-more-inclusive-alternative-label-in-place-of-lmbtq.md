GRSM = gender-, romantic- or sexual minority

I like the GRSM term as an alternative to LMBTQ+. 

LMBTQ / LMBTQAI+ needs constant updates, as further knowledge furthers the inclusion. 

Also there are inclusion-exclusion debates - ie. about polyamorous people. 

GRSM is a broader, more inclusive, yet shorter, simpler and "finished" term.
