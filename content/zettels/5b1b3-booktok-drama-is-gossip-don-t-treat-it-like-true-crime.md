Meredith Novaco youtuber said in her reaction to a "booktok drama" that it's important to keep in mind that 

- we love to chew on these dramas, because they don't leave us with that sick to our stomach feeling (like true crime - my note),
- but we need not to react in such proportions to them like if they would be those sickening dramas. 

These are just pure gossip.

# Related

- Context: she reacted to the Milo/Audra Winter's Age of Scorpius drama. 
- Internet gossip needs to be treaded carefully because the capacity of the internet is vastly different than the capacity of a random person getting involved in it.
	- Like when Paul Castle's 3M following base started harassing a woman having 800 followers. Another booktok drama. 
- Monica Lewinsky said in conversation with John Oliver that she wishes her scandal with Bill Clinton happened in the internet age. Because she was all over the TV and tabloids, but only later, in the internet age did people reach out to say "damn girl, I feel for you, this must have been hard". She said this would've made it easier for her when the scandal happened.
