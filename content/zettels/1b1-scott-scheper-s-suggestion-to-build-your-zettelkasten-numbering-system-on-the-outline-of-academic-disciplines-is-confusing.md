[[ref-scheper-scott|Scott Scheper]], who markets himself as a [[hub-zettelkasten|Zettelkasten]] guru, suggests to build the UID numbering system of your notes collection based on the [Outline of Academic Disciplines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Outline_of_academic_disciplines). 

This idea of using pre-set categories, is contrary to Zettelkasten principles, and it
- a) destroys the concept if applied consistently later on, or
- b) brings unnecessary confusion to the project

It was really confusing for me, when I tried to start from there, but apply the Zettelkasten principle of filing a new idea not in a category where it fits but next to a card to that it's most similar to. 

So I strongly suggest not to start there, but rather check out [[ref-doto-bob|Bob Doto]]'s guide of starting from card 1 being a niche idea: [[1b-the-first-card-of-a-zettelkasten-is-usually-niche]]
