My name is Timi and this is my public Zettelkasten.

You can look at [[hub-zettelkasten|Zettelkasten]] as a micro-blog or a personal wiki. A collection of single-idea, interconnected notes, that come from my own learning, thinking and experiences. 

You can start accessing it from two ways:

- by its topics
- by the trains of thought as they started to form in it

## Browse by topics

As themes emerge in my notes, or there is a topic I know I want to take more notes in the future, I create hubs from where I can access notes of a similar topic. These I call hubs.

To start discovering my notes from a topical entry hub, go to my OmniHub note: [[0a-omnihub]]

(Or just take a look at the hubs section in the left side menu.)


## Browse by trains of thought 

When I make a new note, I try to insert them next to an existing one that's most similar to it. In the Zettelkasten sphere, it's called the Folgezettel approach. Folgezettel simply means follow-up note. The alphanumeric IDs in the title of the notes help these notes to stick together in a list. 

This way, notes form a train of thought by ideas and their follow-up ideas. These trains of thoughts can be a bit more random tho than topical collections, since they connect the way we play a game of association. 

To start discovering my notes not topically, but sifting through trains of thought, go to the root of my Zettelkasten: [[0-root]]

(Or just take a look at the zettels section in the left side menu.)
